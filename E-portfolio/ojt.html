My OJT Journey

From February 24 to April 15, 2025, I completed my On-the-Job Training at the ICT Unit of the University of Rizal System - Morong Campus as a BS Computer Science student. Throughout my training, I was involved in a variety of real-world technical and clerical tasks that enhanced both my skills and confidence in the IT field.

My responsibilities included troubleshooting and maintaining computers, printers, and internet connections across various offices, as well as installing software such as MS Office and PuTTY. I also installed and organized network cables in multiple buildings, cleaned firewalls, and managed technical requests from departments like the Registrar, Dental Clinic, MIS Office, and more. Additionally, I handled document delivery and reception, performed quality assurance, and maintained logs for equipment borrowing and service tracking.

One of the most significant parts of my OJT was being solely responsible for designing and coding a website for the ICT Unit.
